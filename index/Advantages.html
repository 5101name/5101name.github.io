CRISPR applications in cancer treatment

- The reduction of research cost: over the years CRISPR-Cas9 research cost has decremented substantially, which meant that it is in inexpensive to remove a gene or a region, silencing otherwise known as altering the gene.

- The creation of more effective cancer medications: While researching and experimenting on new immunotherapies to cure cancer, it is important that in the median term effective cancer medication be created to slow down the spread of cancer within the human body.
